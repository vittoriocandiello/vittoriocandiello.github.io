Lattice Boltzmann Solver

Minimal LBM solver in C, built while working at Dassault Systemes on acoustic liner boundary conditions for jet engine applications.

The public repository implements a D2Q9 solver with solid wall boundary conditions. No dependencies; outputs can be visualized with ParaView. The full version with time-domain impedance boundary conditions for acoustic liners is not public.
